My Story

I am Carla and I am a creative. I always have been; from various crafting projects to running my own small paper-flower business. In fact, developing and nurturing my creativity has been my side hustle for years.
After a degree and almost a decade of teaching and management experience, I have enjoyed creating engaging and exciting ‘out-of-the-box’ lessons for my students. However, I realised that this was not my life career. I have now studied and found my overwhelming passion for taking my creativity into the digital world of UI/UX and graphic design.
I enjoy the design process and seeing my ideas come to life in the final stages. I enjoy being challenged to find solutions to design problems and taking on user feedback to improve the flow of my work. I enjoy collaborating with others to create something simple, beautiful and functional.
